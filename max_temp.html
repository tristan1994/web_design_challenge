Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator(0 Degree Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of the year than the norther hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year that data was gathered.
